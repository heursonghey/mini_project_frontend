Our Mission

Khmer Heritage Hub is dedicated to preserving and sharing Cambodia’s rich cultural identity. From the ancient temples of Angkor to the vibrant streets of Phnom Penh, we celebrate the traditions, cuisine, and festivals that make Khmer culture truly extraordinary. Our goal is to educate, inspire, and connect people around the world with the beauty of Cambodia.
